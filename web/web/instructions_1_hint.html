The task is to annotate teacher moves for a specific sentence in math tutoring dialogues. It is a one-to-one conversation where the teacher is guiding a 6th-grade student to solve a math word problem. The criteria are:

Grammatical:

The hint is linguistically and grammatically correct.

Specific:

The hint is specific to the step, and not just a vague lead-on statement.

Helpful:

The hint can potentially help the student make progress in the current step, given the previous student's utterance.

Not revealing:

The hint does not reveal the final answer for the step.
